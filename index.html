Sprint 2: Riding the momentum

Sprint 1 showed us what is possible. Sprint 2 is about making it real and lasting. We are building department capability at scale, turning prototypes into tools people actually use every day, and extending that productivity lift to our local product teams too.

Track A
Build capability at scale
Create the knowledge infrastructure that lets everyone move faster, and powers AI agents for local teams.
Track B
Make it production-ready
Finetune our Sprint 1 builds so they are polished, tested, and woven into how we work every single day.
Track C
Uplift our stakeholders
Extend the productivity gains to local product teams. What we built for ourselves, we now build for them.
